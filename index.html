About

Governance and policy professional with nine years of experience producing research that informs real decisions — from electoral reform in Nigeria to regional security across the Sahel.

Currently completing an MSc at Niger Delta University, I bring both academic rigour and field experience to complex governance challenges. My work sits at the intersection of democracy, corruption, and peace — combining peer-reviewed research with direct advocacy to drive meaningful change.

I have designed research frameworks, led multi-stakeholder engagements, supervised research teams, and authored publications for internationally recognised journals and think tanks including the Centre for Democracy and Development (CDD-West Africa).
